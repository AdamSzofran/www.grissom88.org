Event Schedule

Friday, July 18th, 2008

6:30 pm Gather at the Heritage Club at 6:30 pm for a Casual Mixer.
Stay and visit with everyone or split out with your old pals and enjoy the new venues downtown Huntsville has to offer.
Cash bar. Casual dress.
The Heritage Club is located at 111 Washington Street NE in downtown Huntsville.

Saturday, July 19th, 2008

10 am - 1 pm We have a Family Picnic from 10 am to 1 pm at the Huntsville Botanical Garden Pavillion.
Bring your favorite picnic basket. We will provide beverages from Coca-Cola and cake from Peggy Ann Bakery!
Children are welcome. Bubbles the Clown will provide entertainment including face painting and balloon animals for children of all ages.
The Huntsville Botanical Garden is located at 4747 Bob Wallace Ave SW in Huntsville.

1 pm - 3 pm School Tour
Stop off at GHS after the picnic and find your old locker or show your family the famous school without windows. Want to breathe in a bit of nostalgia? Go on the tour, they have remodeled a bit but it still smells the same!
In case you forgot (!?), Grissom is located at 7901 Bailey Cove Rd SE in Huntsville.

7 pm At 7 pm our Cocktail Party at the Heritage Club begins! Join us for a fabulous evening re-connecting with friends that started it all.
Drinks and heavy hors d'oeuvres will be provided. Cocktail attire.
Photographer available $25 buys four 4x6 prints delivered 2 weeks after the event. The photographer will accept all major credit cards, checks, and cash and additional prints may be ordered. This is a great chance for an updated couple photo or a group picture reminiscent of Prom.

If you want to start the party now, we are putting together a living scrapbook at Classmates.com and want everyone to be involved. Submit your memories and see them featured at the Reunion.

See you in July,
Reunion Committee
